Why Lernix?

Built for students,
by students.

01
Real Student Reviews
Every review comes from an actual student who took the class. Honest, anonymous, and always school-specific.
02
Better Class Planning
Stop guessing which teacher is better. Compare ratings, difficulty, and workload side-by-side before registration.
03
Career-Focused Decisions
Every class you take should move you closer to your goal. Lernix shows exactly how each class connects to real careers.
04
AI-Powered Safety
Every review is scanned by Claude AI before going live. Honest feedback gets through. Unfair attacks don't.

Your School, Your Data
Ratings are tied to real schools across Washington. The data is always relevant to where you actually go to school.
